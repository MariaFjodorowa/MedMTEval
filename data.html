The data are pairs of sentences and paragraphs from medical texts in Russian and English.

The data format is jsonlines.

Other train data can be used as well, e.g. the data from WMT2020 , WMT2021 .

The answer format is jsonlines with the following structure:

{"translation": {"ru": "мяу", "en": "meow"}}

The answer file should be named as *.jsonl and zipped into *.zip.
